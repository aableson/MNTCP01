---Question
Turn the following system into an augmented matrix.
<br />
\(\begin{array} -10a + 4b &=& 3 -c\\ a + c + b &=& 0\\ 2 + b - c - a &=& a + 4 \end{array}\)
---Type
onechoice
---Answers
~\(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array} -10 &4&-1&3\\1&1&1&0\\2&1&-1&2 \end{array} \right) \)
~\(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array} -10 &4&1&3\\1&1&1&0\\0&1&-1&-2 \end{array} \right) \)
=\(\displaystyle \left( \begin{array} -10 &4&1&3\\1&1&1&0\\-2&1&-1&2 \end{array} \right) \)
---Comment
Remember to keep everything in the same order in each equation


---Question
If \(\textbf{A} = \begin{pmatrix} 2&3\\1&2 \end{pmatrix}, \textbf{B} = \begin{pmatrix} -1&-1\\2&-3 \end{pmatrix}\) and \(\overrightarrow{v} = \begin{bmatrix}-2\\-1\end{bmatrix}\), find \(\textbf{B}\textbf{A}\textbf{B}\overrightarrow{v}\).
---Type
onechoice
---Answers
=\(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix} -4\\3 \end{bmatrix} \)
~\(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix} 4\\ -3 \end{bmatrix} \)
~\(\displaystyle \begin{bmatrix} 0\\0 \end{bmatrix} \)
---Comment
Remember that order of matrix and vector multiplication really matters!
